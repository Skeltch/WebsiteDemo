Formula 1

There were too many good photos to use for Formula 1 so most of these pages will be of Formula 1. My favorite team would be Red Bull Racing with Daniel Ricciardo and Max Verstappen. The engineering, strategy, and endurance required in Formula 1 is astounding. With new limitations set every year the engineers of each team find incredible ways to compete. Today's V6 Engines are arguably not as loud and powerful sounding, however the hybrid drive train is incredibly powerful and adds a new complexity and strategy. This along with the Drag Reduction System allow for incredible plays. The amount of stress put on the cars, tires, and drivers create an incredible environment of innovation. This page is dedicated to Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg from Mercedes AMG Petronas because these were some great pictures. Most pictures were obtained from this album. Aside from F1, my favorite car would be the McLaren P1, or the Corvette Z06 due to their power and incredible looks.
